About ISOI-VIT

The Instrument Society of India (ISOI), VIT Chapter, is a premier technical club that brings together electronics, instrumentation, and computation to create impactful projects and events.

We provide a platform for students to go beyond textbooks, offering hands-on workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects that bridge hardware and software innovation.
